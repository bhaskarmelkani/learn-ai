## The artifact

Show the shape of the artifact clearly:

- what it contains
- who uses it
- how it gets reviewed

## The workflow

Break the work into steps the learner could repeat on their own.

## Failure modes

- Where does this workflow usually break?
- What signals tell you the artifact is weak?
- Which parts need human review?

<Exercise title="Draft the deliverable">
Ask the learner to produce a lightweight version of the artifact for a problem they care about.
</Exercise>
