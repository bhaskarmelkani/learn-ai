Generative AI is now wider than text.

Common modality pairs include:

| Input | Output | Common job |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Text | Image | concept art, mockups, visual ideation |
| Audio | Text | transcription, meeting notes |
| Text | Audio | narration, voice response |
| Image | Text | captioning, inspection, explanation |
| Document | Structured fields | extraction, classification, Q&A |
| Text | Video | short scene generation, storyboard drafts |

The practical beginner lesson is not "one model can do everything."

It is:

> Different modalities are strong at different jobs, and each one needs its own review habit.

## What Multimodal Systems Are Often Good At

- transcribing speech
- describing or summarizing images
- extracting information from forms and PDFs
- generating rough visual concepts quickly
- turning voice or text into first-draft media assets

## Where They Still Need Care

- image realism can hide factual impossibility
- transcription can miss names or numbers
- document extraction can fail on messy layouts
- video output can look polished while staying shallow or inconsistent
